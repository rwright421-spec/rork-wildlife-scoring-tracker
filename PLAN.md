# Wildlife Spotter — Family Road Trip Animal Scoring App


## Features

- **Start & end trips** — Tap to begin a new trip, and end it when you arrive. Each trip is saved with its date and duration.
- **Add players** — Add 2–4 family members to each trip so everyone can track their own sightings.
- **Score animal sightings** — During an active trip, tap animal buttons (Deer = 1pt, Coyote = 5pt, Bear = 25pt, Cougar = 50pt) under each player to record sightings. Scores update in real time.
- **Custom animals** — Add your own animals with custom point values beyond the default four.
- **Pick a trip winner** — When a trip ends, choose who won that trip.
- **Trip history** — Browse past trips with dates, players, scores, and the winner.
- **Lifetime stats** — See each player's per-day scoring average, total lifetime points, total wins, and animal counts.

## Design

- **Earthy & natural palette** — Deep forest green (#1B4332) as the primary color, warm tan/khaki (#D4A574) as accents, cream (#FDF6EC) backgrounds, and rich brown (#5C3D2E) for text.
- **Textured, outdoorsy feel** — Rounded cards with subtle shadows that feel like field journal entries.
- **Animal icons** — Each animal type gets a distinctive emoji icon for quick recognition (🦌 🐺 🐻 🦁).
- **Score tapping** — Large, satisfying tap targets for adding sightings during bumpy car rides. Subtle haptic feedback on each tap.
- **Leaderboard style** — Active trip scores displayed as a ranked list with the leader highlighted.
- **Stats dashboard** — Clean stat cards showing averages, win counts, and lifetime totals.

## Screens

1. **Home** — Shows the current active trip (if any) with live scores, or a "Start New Trip" button. Also shows a quick summary of recent trips.
2. **Active Trip** — The main scoring screen. Each player has a row with animal buttons to tap. Running totals update live. A button to end the trip and pick a winner.
3. **Trip History** — A scrollable list of past trips showing date, players, scores, and who won.
4. **Trip Detail** — Tap a past trip to see the full breakdown of each player's sightings and points.
5. **Stats** — Lifetime stats per player: average points per day, total wins, lifetime point count, and animal sighting counts.
6. **Settings** — Manage players (add/remove family members) and manage custom animals (add new animals with point values).
